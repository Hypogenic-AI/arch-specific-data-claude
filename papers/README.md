# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers on Attractors in Neural Networks

1. **Concept Attractors in LLMs and their Applications** (`2601.11575_concept_attractors_llms.pdf`)
   - Authors: Chytas, Singh (UW-Madison)
   - Year: 2025 (arXiv Dec 2025)
   - arXiv: 2601.11575
   - Why relevant: Shows LLM layers implement Iterated Function Systems (IFS) with concept-specific attractors. Training-free methods operate on attractors for downstream tasks.

2. **Unveiling Attractor Cycles in LLMs: Successive Paraphrasing** (`2502.15208_attractor_cycles_paraphrasing.pdf`)
   - Authors: Wang, Li, Yan, Cheng, Zhang
   - Year: 2025 (ACL 2025)
   - arXiv: 2502.15208
   - Why relevant: Demonstrates 2-period attractor cycles in LLM paraphrasing. Key gap: only tests transformers, not RNNs.

3. **Comparing Generalization: Transformer vs. RNN in Attractor Dynamics** (`2311.10763_transformer_vs_rnn_attractor_dynamics.pdf`)
   - Authors: Fukushima, Tani (OIST)
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2311.10763
   - Why relevant: **Most directly relevant** - compares how RNN and Transformer learn attractor dynamics. RNN generalizes with 2-4 examples; Transformer needs 25-50.

## Architectural Comparison Papers

4. **RNNs Are Not Transformers (Yet): In-Context Retrieval** (`2402.18510_rnns_not_transformers_yet_iclr2025.pdf`)

   - Year: 2024 (ICLR 2025)
